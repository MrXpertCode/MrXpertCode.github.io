Tips for Maintaining Good Eye Health

  • Regular eye check-ups are essential.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in Vitamin A and antioxidants.
  • Practice eye exercises to reduce strain.
  • Limit screen time and take frequent breaks.
  • Protect your eyes from UV rays.
